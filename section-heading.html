Section Headers/Section Heroes

Section Header Base Component

Allows inserting a page header that requires in addition to the title the subtitle. Offers image, secondary line of h2 text and h3 line. - all of which are not rendered if the corresponding fields are empty.

  •                 
                <div class="score-document-header-with-subtitle (custom class)">
                  <div class="score-header-image">
                    <img src="..." class="(image style)" alt="#"/>
                  </div>
                  <h1>...
                    <br/>
                  </h1>
                  <h2>...</h2>
                </div>

Section Hero Base Component

Allows inserting a page header. Offers image, secondary line of h2 text, h3, body text(Rich text editor), and a call to action. - all of which are not rendered if the corresponding fields are empty.

  •                     
                    <div class="score-section-hero (custom class)" style="">
                      <div class="score-hero-image">
                        <img src="..." alt="...">
                      </div>
                      <h2>
                        Title Line 1 <br/> Title Line 2 </h2>
                      <h3>Hero Subtitle</h3>
                      <div class="score-hero-body">
                        <!--hero body-->
                      </div>
                      <div class="score-call-to-action">
                        <!--call to action-->
                      </div>
                    </div>
