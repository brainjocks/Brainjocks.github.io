Slick Slider

Slick Slider Base Component

Score slick slider is a container to collect slick slider panels for a slick slider of information The design and text of the panels will be a composition of other components.

  • 
<div class="score-slick-slider
 (amount-of-slides-to-show-class)
 slick-slider slick-dotted" id="">

  <!--previous button-->
  <button class="slick-prev slick-arrow"
          aria-label="Previous"
          type="button"
          style="">Previous</button>
  <!--container-->
  <div class="slick-list draggable">
    <div class="slick-track">
      <!--Slick Slider Panel components-->
      <div class="score-slick-slider-panel
      slick-slide (slick-cloned | slick-current | slick-active)"
           data-slick-index="..."
           aria-hidden="true|false">
        <!--any content-->
        ...
      </div>
      ...
    </div>
  </div>
  <!--next button-->
  <button class="slick-next slick-arrow"
          aria-label="Next"
          type="button"
          style="">Next</button>
  <!--pager-->
  <ul class="slick-dots" role="tablist">
    <li role="presentation">
      <button type="button"
              role="tab"
              id="..."
              aria-controls="..."
              aria-label="1 of x">1</button>
    </li>
    ...
    <li role="presentation">
      <button type="button"
              role="tab" id="..."
              aria-controls="..."
              aria-label="x of x"
              aria-selected="...">x</button>
    </li>
  </ul>
</div>
