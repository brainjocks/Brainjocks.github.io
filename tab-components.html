Tab Base Component

A Tabset is a container that consists of tab panels A Tab is the child rendering for the tabset representing a single panel of content.

  •                   
                  <div class="score-tab (custom class)" id="...">
                  <div class="nav-tabs-wrapper-outer">
                    <div class="nav-tabs-wrapper-inner">
                      <ul class="nav nav-tabs" role="tablist">
                        <!-- tab links -->
                      </ul>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="tab-content-wrapper-outer">
                    <div class="tab-content-wrapper-inner">
                      <div class="tab-content">
                        <!-- tab panel component -->
                        <div id="..." class="tab-pane score-tab-panel
                        (active) (custom class)">
                          ...
                        </div>
                      </div>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                </div>
